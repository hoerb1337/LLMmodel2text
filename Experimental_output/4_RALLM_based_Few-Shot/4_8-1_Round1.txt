The HR Department checks the application in the "check application" task. If the application is approved, the HR Department fills the post in the "fill post" task.

The process ends with an end event in the Functional Department's lane when the post is filled. The end event is annotated with the text "contract is signed", indicating that the recruitment process is completed when a contract is signed with the new hire.
